    About

    Hello.

    I'm Yusuke — a name that, in Japanese, means “to help, to be of use.” I built Kotodama because I felt my own name had been quietly carrying me through life, long before I learned to listen.

    The Japanese have a word for this: kotodama (言霊) — “the spirit of words.” Every sound is alive. Names are not arbitrary. What you call yourself shapes how the world receives you — that is the old belief.

    The reading you receive is born from a kotodama dictionary I built, sound by sound. I have woven the old Japanese interpretations and my own understanding into a single system. I am not creating the kotodama — they have always been here. I am only translating, for those who read in English.

    Each Gift Edition is hand-prepared by me, with care, and delivered to your inbox within 24 hours of your order.

    — Yusuke
